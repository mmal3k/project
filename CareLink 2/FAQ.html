For Patients and Families

What is CareLink, and how can it help homebound patients?
CareLink is a platform that connects homebound patients with caregivers and social workers who can provide essential care and support in the comfort of their own homes. Our goal is to improve the quality of life for those who need it most.
Who can benefit from CareLink's services?
CareLink is designed to assist seniors, individuals with disabilities, and children who require in-home care and support.
How do I request a caregiver or social worker?
To request a caregiver or social worker, simply sign up on our platform, provide your details, and describe your specific needs. Then CareLink matches you with a suitable caregiver or social worker.
What services do caregivers and social workers offer?
Caregivers and social workers can assist with various tasks, including personal care, companionship, meal preparation, medication management, and more. You can discuss your specific requirements when requesting a caregiver.
How are caregivers and social workers screened and selected?
CareLink carefully screens and verifies all caregivers and social workers to ensure they meet our quality standards. We conduct background checks and assess qualifications and experience.
How can I trust the caregivers or social workers provided by CareLink?
We prioritize safety and quality. Caregivers and social workers in our network are professionals committed to providing excellent care. You can read reviews and ratings from other users to help you make an informed decision.
How are payments handled?
We offer a secure payment system. You'll be billed based on the services provided, and payments can be made through our platform.

For caregivers

How can I become a caregiver or social worker with CareLink?
If you're a qualified caregiver or social worker, you can apply to join our network. Sign up, provide your credentials, and undergo our screening process.
What types of clients can I expect to work with through CareLink?
CareLink serves a diverse range of clients, including seniors, individuals with disabilities, and children. You may be matched with clients based on your expertise and their needs.
How do I set my availability and accept assignments?
You have the flexibility to set your availability and accept assignments that fit your schedule. Our platform allows you to manage your work hours.
What support and resources does CareLink offer to caregivers and social workers?
CareLink provides training, resources, and a support team to assist caregivers and social workers in delivering the best possible care.
How does payment work for caregivers and social workers?
Caregivers and social workers are compensated for their services through our secure payment system. Payments are based on the services provided according to what price agreed on with the patient.
What if I encounter any issues while providing care?
If you face any challenges while providing care, you can contact the closest family member of the patient else you contact our support team for assistance and guidance only.
